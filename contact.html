Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about our services

What areas do you serve?

We primarily serve Kayole Junction and surrounding areas including Fedha Estate, Soweto, Nyayo Estate, and parts of Embakasi. We're continually expanding our service area.

How quickly can you deliver?

Standard delivery within Kayole Junction takes 1-2 hours. Express delivery is available within 45 minutes for an additional fee. Delivery times to surrounding areas may vary.

What are your payment methods?

We accept cash on delivery, M-Pesa, Airtel Money, and bank transfers. For corporate clients, we offer monthly billing with credit terms.

Do you deliver on weekends and holidays?

Yes, we operate 7 days a week, including public holidays. Our hours on holidays are slightly reduced (8:00 AM - 8:00 PM).

What if my item gets damaged during delivery?

We take great care with all items. In the rare event of damage, please contact us immediately. We have a claims process and insurance options for valuable items.
