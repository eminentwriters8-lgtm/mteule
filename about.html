About MTEULE Ventures

Your trusted delivery partner in Kayole Junction since 2018

Our Story

MTEULE Ventures was founded in 2018 by Michael Mteule, a lifelong resident of Kayole Junction. What started as a small bicycle delivery service with just one employee has grown into one of the most trusted delivery companies in the area.

Michael saw a need for reliable, affordable delivery services in Kayole as the community continued to grow. Many residents were spending valuable time traveling to collect packages, food, and groceries when they could be focusing on their work and families.

Today, we serve hundreds of customers daily with a fleet of 15 delivery vehicles and a dedicated team of 25 staff members. Despite our growth, we remain committed to our founding principles: reliability, affordability, and exceptional customer service.

Our Mission

To provide fast, reliable, and affordable delivery services that connect Kayole Junction residents and businesses, making daily life more convenient and productive for our community.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted delivery service provider in Eastlands, Nairobi, by expanding our services while maintaining our commitment to customer satisfaction and community development.

Our Values

Reliability, Integrity, Customer Focus, Community Engagement, and Continuous Improvement guide every aspect of our operations and decision-making.
